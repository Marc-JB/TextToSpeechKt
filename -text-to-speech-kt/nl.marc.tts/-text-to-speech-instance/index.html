Behaviour of this method:1A) clearQueue is true: Clears the internal queue (like the stop method). 1B) clearQueue is false: Retains the internal queue.2A) isMuted is true, or volume is zero: No text is added to the queue. 2B) isMuted is false and volume is above zero: Adds the text with volume, rate and pitch to the internal queue.
